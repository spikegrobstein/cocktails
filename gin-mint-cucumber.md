# Gin Cucumber Mint

> https://cookieandkate.com/cucumber-mint-gimlet-recipe/

## Ingredients

 * 2" of cucumber, halved and quartered
 * 10 mint leaves
 * 1/2 oz simple syrup
 * 3/4 oz lime juice
 * 1.5 oz gin

## Instructions

 1. muddle cucumber, mint and simple syrup in shaker
 2. add rest of ingredients
 3. add ice
 4. shake
 5. strain into glass
