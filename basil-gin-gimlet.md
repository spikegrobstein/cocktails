# Basil Gin Gimlet

> https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/1013650-live-basil-gimlet

## Ingredients

 * 5 large basil leaves
 * 1.5 oz gin
 * 3/4 oz lime juice
 * 1/2 oz simple syrup

## Instructions

 1. Muddle basil and simple syrup
 2. add gin, lime juice
 3. add ice
 4. shake
 5. strain into glass
